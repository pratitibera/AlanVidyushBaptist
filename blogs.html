Dr. Sandeep Gupta is an eye specialist who went from fat to shredded in 6 months under my guidance while following a fully vegan diet. He saw my own transformation pictures on Instagram and decided to reach out for an online consultation.
His transformation is proof of what hard work and guidance of some of the best coaches in the industry can result in.
Fully Vegan Diet
Dr. Sandeep was on a completely vegan diet during his weight loss journey. If you are not familiar with vegan diets, it means that absolutely no animal products, not even milk, eggs, or paneer are consumed.
To make sure his body got proper nutrition and had enough strength for his workouts, special attention was given to ensure his Vitamin B12 and D3 requirements were fulfilled.
There is a common misconception in the fitness community that a vegan diet cannot result in a shredded body with abs, but one look at Dr. Sandeep’s physique is enough to prove them wrong.
Custom Plan
To ensure the best results, we made sure that Sandeep’s workout and diet plans changed every month according to what his body and lifestyle required at the moment.
Each month we assessed the progress he made and how his body was reacting to the provided diets and changed it accordingly. We also provided stretching routines in some cases. This meant that there was no lack of strength and excitement in Sandeep and he was making steady progress towards a ripped and fit body.
That’s the best part of our custom plans, we don’t change the individual to fit a particular diet or workout routine, rather the individual is given utmost priority and a tailor-made routine is created just for them.
Overcoming Doubt
Sandeep lost over 10kgs during his 6 month transformation period, but the journey was far from easy. A few of his distant family members were not supportive of his weight loss journey and unwanted comments on his physique were becoming more and more common.
Understandably, this took a gradual toll on his mental health and confidence levels. There were periods during which he felt really discouraged and his enthusiasm regarding his end goal was slowly waning.
As his trainers, it was also our responsibility to motivate him through constant pep talk and by narrating various other inspirational stories to him of clients who’d been through similar harrowing body shaming experiences that were similar to his and how they overcame the same. This served to boost his morale and he continued to power through the process despite the sharp criticism he regularly faced.
With consistent motivation and reassurances that he was not alone in this, we made sure Sandeep did not stray from his path to becoming his best and fittest. Through our communication, I made him realise that if people noticed the changes in his body, it was a huge sign that the results are exactly how we wanted them.
Online Consultation
Some people may believe that online consultations don’t work, but with dedication and teamwork between the trainer and the client, nothing is impossible.
Sandeep is one of those people who never falter from their goals and work hard consistently and patiently for results. He dealt with both the physical and mental challenges of his fitness journey with incredible strength and determination and serves as an inspiration to anyone who may be too overwhelmed or intimidated to start working towards a fitter body and better health.
He believed in his trainers and our plans and the results are obvious in his amazing transformation. So if you think that proper gains can only be made if your trainer is physically present in front of you, think again.
